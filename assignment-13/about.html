for the love of color


I have been obsessed with color ever since I can remember. Bright colors, pastels, monochrome, I love them all. Inspired heavily by artists such as Lisa Congdon, Ashley Mary, and Minju Kim, I strive to create pieces that not only play with color but also delve into the world of geometry. Each piece is a wearable work of art made with love.
