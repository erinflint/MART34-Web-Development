Why brick stitch?

I have been working at a bead shop for almost four years now and have learned a plethora of beading skills. Brick stitch has proven to be one of my favorites. I decided to write a little instruction page on this stitch because I personally find it to be a lot of fun! It takes a while to complete a piece (my first earrings took fifteen hours!) but I find this technique to be calming and meditative.
