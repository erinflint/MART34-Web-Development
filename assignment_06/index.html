It's Moussaka, not Moose Caca

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 3 medium eggplants
  • 1 large onion
  • 1.5 cups extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 TBSP tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

For the Bechamel Sauce

  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • one stick butter
  • 2 egg yolks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Slice eggplant lenghtwise into quarter-inch thick slices.
  3. Place slices in a large bowl filled with salt water. Let soak for 30 minutes.
  4. Over medium heat, fry eggplant slices in batches in olive oil. Add oil regluarly.
  5. Remove eggplant slices and place on paper towel.
  6. In the same pan, add one quarter cup of oil and fry onions until transluscent.
  7. Add meat, cook for 10 minutes. Add tomato paste.
  8. Grease baking pan with oil.
  9. Cover bottom of pan colpletely with eggplant slices.
  10. Spoon out meat mixture onto eggplant slice layer. Repeat layering process.
Bechamel Sauce
  1. Melt butter in medium saucepan over low heat.
  2. Slowly mix in flour, whisking away clumps.
  3. Once flour is incorporated, gradually add milk while stirring continuously.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Slowly add egg yolks to sauce, making sure to combine well.
  6. Pour a thick layer of Bechamel into pan completely covering the top meat sauce layer.
  7. Place baking pan into oven, bake for 40 mins or until top is nicely browned.
