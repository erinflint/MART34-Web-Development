Ladies of the 70s

The Seventies was a time of big hair, big attitude, and some of the best fashion (in my opinion, at least). I found 3 stylish ladies from that era to use in this assignment: Farrah Fawcett, Cher, and Brigitte Bardot. I chose the text and background colors on this page to compliment their outfits/highlight some of the classic 70s colors.
