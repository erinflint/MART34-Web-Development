for the love of color


I have been obsessed with color ever since I can remember. Bright colors, pastels, monochrome, I love them all. Inspired heavily by artists such as Lisa Congdon, Ashley Mary, and Minju Kim, I strive to create pieces that not only play with color but also delve into the world of geometry. I have been lucky enough to have worked at a gorgeous bead shop for the last four years. In doing so, I have built up a skillset that borrows from many techniques in the jewelry making world. Visit my gallery page to get a glimpse into what I create!
