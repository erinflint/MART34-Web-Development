Bruce Degen

author of Jamberry

Bruce Degen's Jamberry hit the shelves in 1983. Degen is mostly known in the book world as an illustrator. He is most famous for his work illustrating The Magic School Bus written by Joanna Cole. Degen was born in Brooklyn NY in 1945 and pursued his art career from an early age. He found his love of illustration in elementary school and went on to earn a Masters of Fine Arts from the Pratt Institute in Brooklyn.
